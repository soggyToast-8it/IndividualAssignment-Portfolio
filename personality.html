AI Business Ideas Report

Artificial Intelligence is being used to help with repetitive and time-consuming tasks like scheduling, or writing E-mails.

In my life as a University student, the idea of AI is somewhat helpful for me. During finals, studying weeks worth of notes can be very overwhelming. An AI-based productivity tool would help me summarize my notes, and even create questions to practice when there's a scarcity of resources to use as practice.

One major ethical concern is privacy. The amount of personal information that is gathered by AI productivity tools raises questions on how this data is stored, and who has access to it. Another concern is over-reliance. There is concern that humans are too reliant on AI tools; relying on the AI to make decisions and manage their plans, humans will lose the ability think on their own.

Solution

A solution to these problems would be to have the tool be developed with full transparency, and allow the users the right to delete any collected data as they please. The other is to have the AI tool be developed only as a helper, every decision must be confirmed by the user.

Example

An example of this is Notion AI. The Notion app is very popular among students. Notion AI is a user productivity tool that employs AI to assist users in writing, summarizing, and organizing their notes and tasks in one place.
